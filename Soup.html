Just look at it. Can you name all of the ingredients? Where I come from, soup is more than a meal. Soup is medicine. Homemade soup (no disrespect to Campbell's, Progresso, and others) is a delicatessen. It is prescribed to both the sick and the healthy. Soup blends together meat, vegetables, starches, and seasonings into a warm medley of delight. What's better than soup on a cool autumn evening?
